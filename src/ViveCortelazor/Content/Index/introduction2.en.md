#### What to See and Do in Cortelazor

If you’re wondering what to see in **Cortelazor**, the answer combines nature, culture, and tradition.

In the **[what to do in Cortelazor](/en/what-to-do)** section, you’ll find ideas for strolling through its whitewashed streets, discovering scenic viewpoints overlooking the mountains, and enjoying its popular local festivals.

In recent years, a **[network of signposted trails](/en/hiking)** with different levels of difficulty has been developed, making **Cortelazor** a strategic destination for rural tourism in the Sierra de Huelva.

You can find more detailed information about routes and itineraries in the **[hiking in Cortelazor](/en/hiking)** section.

#### Hiking and Nature in the Sierra de Huelva

The natural surroundings of **Cortelazor** are one of its greatest attractions. Holm oaks, cork oaks, and chestnut trees shape a landscape that remains green for much of the year.

The **[Cortelazor trails](/en/hiking)** allow visitors to discover streams, natural rock formations shaped by erosion, and spectacular panoramic views of the Sierra de Aracena. Many routes are circular or connect with nearby villages, expanding the possibilities for those who enjoy walking. Among the most popular routes is the **[Charco Malo Trail](/en/hiking#charco-malo)**.

It is an authentic, peaceful, and well-preserved nature tourism experience.
