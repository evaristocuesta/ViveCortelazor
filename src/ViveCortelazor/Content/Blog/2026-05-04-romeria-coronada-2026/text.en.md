From **May 6th to 10th**, Cortelazor la Real celebrates in honor of its patron saint, **Our Lady of the Coronada**. Five days full of devotion, tradition, and joy, culminating on Sunday with the long-awaited pilgrimage (Romería).

![Programacion Romería Nuestra Señora de la Coronada Cortelazor](/images/blog/2026-05-04-romeria-coronada-2026/cartel.jpg)

Below is the **full schedule of events**:

## Wednesday, May 6th

**8:30 PM**
First triduum mass and floral offering to Our Lady of the Coronada.

## Thursday, May 7th

**8:30 PM**
Second triduum mass and **Proclamation (Pregón)** delivered by **Elisabel Pérez Gómez**.

## Friday, May 8th

**8:30 PM**
Third triduum mass and **ceremonial handover of the Mayordomo staff**:

* Outgoing: **Ana Sánchez Sánchez**
* Incoming: **Begoña Vázquez Sánchez**

The mass will be sung by the group **"Familia González"** from Los Marines.

## Saturday, May 9th

**10:00 PM**
**Holy Rosary** to the Blessed Virgin of the Coronada.

## Sunday, May 10th

**7:30 AM**
Fireworks salute and traditional morning music (“Alegre Diana”) led by the drummer **Félix de Carboneras**.

**8:00 AM**
Departure of the Simpecado towards the Hermitage, accompanied by the band **Grupo del Cerro**.
A minute of silence will be observed when passing by the cemetery.

**12:00 PM**
Arrival of the Simpecado at the Hermitage and **welcome of visiting brotherhoods**.

**1:00 PM**
**Pilgrimage Mass** officiated by parish administrator **Eliécer López Cardona**, with music by the choir **Ntra. Sra. de la Fuente**.
Afterwards, **procession of the Blessed Virgin**.

**6:30 PM**
Return of the Simpecado to the village.

**10:00 PM**
DJ party at Plaza de Andalucía.
