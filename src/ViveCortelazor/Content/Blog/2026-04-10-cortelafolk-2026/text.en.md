The **Cortelafolk 2026** lineup is finally here — and it couldn’t look better. Three days to come together, enjoy, and fill the village with music, culture, and great vibes in the heart of the Sierra de Huelva 🌿

**Dates:** April 17, 18, and 19
**Location:** Cortelazor (Sierra de Huelva, Spain)
**Entry:** FREE!

![III Cortelafolk festival folk Cortelazor 2026](/images/blog/2026-04-10-cortelafolk-2026/cartel.jpg)

## What can you expect this year?

* More than 10 concerts across 2 stages
* Morning batucada (percussion parade)
* Fandango documentary screening
* Dance, yoga, and games workshops
* Food, drinks, and artisan stalls
* And above all… an amazing atmosphere!

## A festival with soul

Cortelafolk is not just about music. It’s an experience where culture, nature, and community come together in one of the most beautiful villages in the Sierra.

You can also enjoy the festival’s food stalls or explore the local cuisine by visiting the [bars and restaurants in Cortelazor](/en/where-to-eat).

A perfect plan to disconnect, discover new artists, and enjoy the unique atmosphere of Cortelazor.
