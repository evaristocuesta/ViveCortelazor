## Activities at Plaza de Andalucía and Plaza del Olmo

Cortelazor is once again preparing to celebrate a December long weekend full of activities, solidarity, and a festive Christmas atmosphere. On December 6 and 7, Plaza de Andalucía and Plaza del Olmo will become the gathering point for both residents and visitors.

Below, you can find all the events planned.

![December in Cortelazor](/images/blog/2025-12-01-puente-diciembre-cortelazor/cartel.jpg)

---

## December 6 · Plaza de Andalucía

Starting at **5:00 p.m.**, an afternoon full of activities for all ages will begin.

### III Solidarity Market of Crafts and Second-Hand Items

From **3:00 p.m. to 7:00 p.m.**, Plaza del Olmo will host the third edition of the Solidarity Market, a space where artisans and neighbors can offer handmade items and second-hand goods.
This market helps support the care and feeding of the village’s cat colonies.

### Buñuelos with hot chocolate

Prepared to raise funds for the **Coronada Brotherhood**.

### Flamenco Zambomba *Carita Divina*

A live performance with traditional Christmas songs to fill the evening with festive spirit.

### Christmas lighting ceremony

Official switching-on of the Christmas lights, accompanied by artificial snow to welcome the holiday season.

---

## December 7 · Plaza de Andalucía

### Sweets in support of the Remedios Brotherhood

From **4:00 p.m.**, various traditional sweets will be available, with proceeds benefiting the Remedios Brotherhood.

### Mass and procession

At **6:00 p.m.**, a mass in honor of **Our Lady of the Immaculate Conception** will be held.
Afterwards, the procession will take place through the streets of the village.

---

## Organized and supported by

* La Mano Verde
* Brotherhood of Our Lady of the Remedios
* Brotherhood of Our Lady of the Coronada
* Cortelazor Town Council
* Women’s Association “La Palomera”

---

Cortelazor once again demonstrates its community spirit and solidarity with a festive long weekend full of tradition, craftsmanship, and shared activities. It is the perfect opportunity to enjoy the village atmosphere and support local initiatives.
