## Saturday, August 23 · 7:00 p.m. · El Gallo Negro · Cortelazor

Next **Saturday, August 23**, starting at **7:00 p.m.**, El Gallo Negro Cortelazor hosts an evening dedicated to **World Music**, where music becomes a bridge between cultures.

![World Music Party](/images/blog/2025-08-22-fiesta-world-music/fiesta-world-music.jpg)

## DJ Miss Underground
Anthropologist and social educator, **Dj Miss Underground** has spent years exploring African music and its diasporas. Her passion for **hip hop, funk, souljazz, and afrobeat** is reflected in sessions with a strong critical and social perspective.  

Through her mixes, she advocates for **alternative ways of understanding the world**, questioning Western cultural hegemony and giving voice to diverse perspectives.

## Nachevere
The evening will also feature **Nachevere**, in a **B2B** that will bring together different musical sensibilities, creating a sonic dialogue between tradition and modernity.
