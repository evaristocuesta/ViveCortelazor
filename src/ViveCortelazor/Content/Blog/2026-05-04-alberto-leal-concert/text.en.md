## Saturday, May 9 · 9:00 PM · La Mano Verde · Cortelazor

**Admission: €10 / €8 for members**

This Saturday, May 9 at 9:00 PM, **La Mano Verde** welcomes **Alberto Leal** to **Cortelazor**, accompanied by the great guitarist **Miguelo Delgado**.

![Alberto Leal Concert La Mano Verde](/images/blog/2026-05-04-alberto-leal-concert/cartel.jpg)

## An intimate and emotional live performance

Alberto Leal’s music blends **sensitivity and strength**, with honest songs and lyrics that connect on a deep level. His style stands out for its intimacy and care, where each piece invites you to let yourself go.

Accompanied by Miguelo Delgado’s guitar, the concert promises a **warm and special atmosphere**, the kind that leaves a lasting impression and is enjoyed from beginning to end.
